Our Tail-Wagging Story

We believe everyone deserves the joy of a pet, regardless of their living situation or schedule.

Why we started

Founded in 2024, RentAPet began with a simple observation: millions of people love animals but can't commit to full-time ownership due to work, travel, or apartment rules.


At the same time, many socialized pets thrive on extra adventure and human interaction. We built a bridge between the two, focusing on safety, animal welfare, and pure happiness.
